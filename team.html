Meet The Team

This is the 2025 Wells Cathedral School entry for the Geological Society's 'Schools Geology Challenge' Geological storylines or locations feature in numerous movies; however, sometimes, their geological content can be unrealistic. For our entry, we wanted to highlight the good, the bad and the ugly aspects of geology in movies.
